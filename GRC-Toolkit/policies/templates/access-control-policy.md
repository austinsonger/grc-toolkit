# Access Control Policy Template

## 1. Purpose
Define minimum access control requirements to protect organizational systems and data.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all workforce users, contractors, service accounts, and third parties accessing company-managed systems.

## 3. Policy Statements
1. All access must be role-based and approved by data/system owners.
2. Privileged access must be granted only for approved administrative duties.
3. Access reviews must be performed at least quarterly.
4. Access must be revoked within 24 hours of termination or role change.
5. MFA is required for all privileged and remote access.

## 4. Control Mapping
- `CTRL-AC-001`
- NIST: `AC-2`, `AC-3`
- ISO 27001: `A.5.15`, `A.5.18`
- SOC 2: `CC6.1`, `CC6.2`

## 5. Evidence
- Access request approvals
- Access review records
- Deprovisioning tickets and IAM logs
